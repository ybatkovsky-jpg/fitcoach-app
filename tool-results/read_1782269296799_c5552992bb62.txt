    53‚Üí    82‚Üí    82‚ÜíSubmaximal Effort Training
    54‚Üí    83‚Üí    83‚ÜíThe repeated effort method, covered in chapter 3, is an integral aspect
    55‚Üí    84‚Üí    84‚Üíof every program in this book. While there are some similarities between
    56‚Üí    85‚Üí    85‚Üíit and the submaximal effort method, the major difference is that
    57‚Üí    86‚Üí    86‚Üísubmaximal work is delivered exclusively through bilateral movements
    58‚Üí    87‚Üí    87‚Üí(i.e., squats, presses, pulls). The submaximal effort method will be
    59‚Üí    88‚Üí    88‚Üíused as a multiple-repetition maximum scenario (two- to six-rep max)
    60‚Üí    89‚Üí    89‚Üíbecause true maximal effort training consists of a singular one-rep
    61‚Üí    90‚Üí    90‚Üímaximal load set.
    62‚Üí    91‚Üí    91‚ÜíThe goal with the submaximal effort method is twofold: (1) to develop
    63‚Üí    92‚Üí    92‚Üíproper motor patterns with bilateral movements approaching heavier
    64‚Üí    93‚Üí    93‚Üíloads and (2) to improve strength qualities via recruitment of high-
    65‚Üí    94‚Üí    94‚Üíthreshold motor units and thus improve neuromuscular efficiency.
    66‚Üí    95‚Üí    95‚ÜíFurthermore, this method is a prerequisite for maximal effort training,
    67‚Üí    96‚Üí    96‚Üímeaning that one should have first developed efficient movement
    68‚Üí    97‚Üí    97‚Üíbefore considering attempting maximal effort lifts. Moreover, the
    69‚Üí    98‚Üí    98‚Üísubmaximal effort method can be used strategically for autoregulation.
    70‚Üí    99‚Üí    99‚ÜíStress affects all individuals differently, and asking someone to adhere
    71‚Üí   100‚Üí   100‚Üíto rigid percentages (five sets of three reps at 75 percent of one-rep
    72‚Üí   101‚Üí   101‚Üímax) does not account for daily fluctuations. Using submaximal effort
    73‚Üí   102‚Üí   102‚Üítraining allows the individual to build up in weight based on how they
    74‚Üí   103‚Üí   103‚Üíare feeling on any given day. This method is also easier to navigate
    75‚Üí   104‚Üí   104‚Üíbecause building to a heavy triple for the day is less confusing than
    76‚Üí   105‚Üí   105‚Üíadhering to strict percentage-based guidelines. Submaximal effort
    77‚Üí   106‚Üí   106‚Üítraining works both aspects of the force-velocity curve while creating
    78‚Üí   107‚Üí   107‚Üíbalanced programming to reduce the risk of overtraining.
